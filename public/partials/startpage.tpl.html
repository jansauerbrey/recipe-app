Recipe Planner

Browse, collect and organize your recipes, cooking schedules and shopping lists in the ultimative Recipe Planner.

Recipes

View all your recipes and discover new recipes created by other users. Mark favorite recipes and share cooking inspirations with your friends. Explore new recipes as well as find specific recipes via category and ta search.

Schedule

Plan your recipes and add them to individual days in the future. Number of persons will be used to calculate amount for shopping list. All ingredients will be added to the shopping list for easy shopping without hassle.

Shopping

View all necessary ingredients of items added automatically through scheduling or added manually. Overview gives the posibillity to quickly add most frequent added items. Shoping list will stay in sync accross devices.
